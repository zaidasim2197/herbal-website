About Us

We are undergraduates of the Pharm D program at Dow University of Health Sciences (DCOP, DUHS) aiming to gain the best knowledge and share it with the ambition of improving health outcomes. After extensive study of herbal ingredients and their awareness among the population, we observed increased interest but limited knowledge. This inspired us to initiate this research project with the intention of making herbal knowledge accessible to everyone.

We evaluated the need for a digital tool that can offer precise, easily comprehensible, and accessible information about herbal ingredients. This project envisions bringing trustworthy herbal knowledge to your fingertips.

Our Vision

This website aims to encourage the safe and informed use of herbal ingredients using pharmacovigilance principles. It bridges the gap between traditional knowledge and modern science by acting as a comprehensive resource. Our platform provides detailed, evidence-based information including indications, contraindications, and potential side effects of commonly used herbal ingredients — all made easily accessible to the public.
